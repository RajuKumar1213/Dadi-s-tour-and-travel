Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to the most common questions about our services

How can I book a vehicle?

You can book a vehicle through our website, by calling our booking number, or by visiting our office. We recommend booking in advance to ensure availability.

What documents are required for booking?

For booking a vehicle, you'll need a valid ID proof, driving license (if self-driving), and advance payment. For international customers, a passport copy is required.

What is your cancellation policy?

Cancellations made 48 hours before the scheduled trip receive a full refund. Cancellations between 24-48 hours receive a 50% refund, and cancellations within 24 hours are non-refundable.

Do you provide drivers with your vehicles?

Yes, we provide experienced drivers with all our vehicles. All our drivers are professionally trained and familiar with routes across the country.

Can I book a vehicle for outstation travel?

Yes, we offer outstation travel services. You can book any of our vehicles for outstation trips, including multi-city tours and long-distance journeys.
