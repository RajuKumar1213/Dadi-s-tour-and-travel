About Sunny Travels

Your trusted partner for all transportation needs since 2010

Our Story

Founded in 2010, Sunny Travels began with a modest fleet of just 5 vehicles serving the local community. What started as a small family business has now grown into one of the most trusted vehicle rental services across the country.

Our founder, Mr. Sunil Sharma, had a vision to create a transportation service that prioritized customer satisfaction, safety, and reliability above all else. This vision remains at the core of our business values today.

Over the years, we have expanded our fleet to include over 200 vehicles of various types and capacities, allowing us to serve a diverse range of transportation needs from individual travel to large group events.

Our Mission & Vision

Guided by strong principles, we strive to be the leader in transportation services

Our Mission

To provide safe, reliable, and comfortable transportation solutions to our customers while maintaining the highest standards of service excellence and environmental responsibility.

We are committed to making travel accessible, affordable, and enjoyable for everyone, whether it's a daily commute or a special occasion.

Our Vision

To be the most trusted and preferred transportation service provider in India, recognized for our exceptional customer service, diverse fleet, and innovative transportation solutions.

We aspire to revolutionize the way people travel by constantly evolving and integrating sustainable practices into our operations.
